Title: The next era of DeFi will be defined by TVC (Total Value Covered), not TVL (Total Value Locked).
URL: https://x.com/Firelightfi/status/2028491218943156415
Hidden Assumption: Total Value Locked (TVL) is the most important metric for measuring the success and security of a DeFi protocol. More TVL equals a better protocol.
Systemic Gap: TVL is a vanity metric that measures the quantity of capital but ignores its quality, risk, and durability. It incentivizes protocols to attract mercenary capital with unsustainable yields, creating systemic fragility. It fails to account for the actual capital at risk vs. capital that is hedged, insured, or otherwise covered.
Required Primitive: A new DeFi metric standard, "Total Value Covered" (TVC), that measures the amount of capital within a protocol that is explicitly protected against specific risks (e.g., smart contract failure, de-pegging). This requires on-chain insurance and risk-management primitives to become a core part of the DeFi stack.
Recommended Research Leads: Formalize the definition of TVC. Create dashboards that track both TVL and TVC for major protocols. Research the economic incentives required to bootstrap on-chain insurance markets and make "covered capital" a core feature of lending and yield platforms.
Stance: support
Reason: This directly challenges one of DeFi's most foundational (and flawed) metrics. Shifting focus from "locked" capital to "covered" capital represents a major paradigm shift from a growth-obsessed, high-risk culture to a mature, risk-managed financial system. This is essential for attracting the next wave of risk-averse institutional users.
